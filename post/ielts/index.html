第三部份

在IELTS的第三部分呢，通常会基于你问题二的答案上进一步回答考官的问题。

这就考验了你对问题二的进一步理解（联系到生活，更加口语化地解释一遍）。

他们在这part会严格掌控时间。

在pt.2的时候 这个topic是a home you don't wanna live in，你会有你的ideas about the home

举个栗子：

where it is? 我aunt的房子

这里前面可以先引出答案：Well, I’m a travel freak and I love to explore different places. I love to travel with my friends to exciting tourist destinations or to my relatives. 进一步引出回答：However, the most common problem that I usually face is living at any place for more then 2 days. As it becomes really tiring and uncomfortable. 是一个房子: So, the place is my maternal aunt’s house in Guangzhou, and I have been visiting her every year since I was ten years old.

what it is like & why you visted it?

大房子，因为我每个暑假都会去玩

When I visted GZ then I am always stay my aunt's place with my familes, which is located in the center of the city in Guangzhou.

My aunt live there with my cousins and her husband, it was a well decoreted big house that nearby Central Park.

Similarly, I love to visit my relatives house as we have a lot of fun and do various activities like go for **Window shopping, watch films and so on. ** 玩这些咯
Despite that it becomes quite difficult for me to stay there for a long time due to various reasons.过渡到最后一个问题

why you don't like to live there?

Firstly, I don’t like if anyone gets disturb because of me. As its a fact that when any relative visits the whole schedule of the host hinders. 我不喜欢给人添麻烦
Secondly, they do a lot of formalities like cook 2 to 3 dishes as well as something sweet and that not only for one day but till the date I live with them.食物不合口味
Most importantly, it does not matter that your house is huge or small, it’s the only place where a person gets satisfaction and gets relaxed after working a full day.
Apart from this, I do have some responsibilities towards my home due to which I can’t stay away from it for a long time. It is difficult for me to go out with friends in GZ while I am staying there.住在他们家就得保持礼貌 要社交 要留在屋子里面 假如想出去玩不能想走就走

Besides, my aunt's family plus myself's are great numbers of people living in the same house.

The house is always a bit noisy in the daytime. My cousins one is 6 and the other is 12. They like screaming, playing around me. I cannot even read a book or finish a movie. 熊孩子太鸡儿吵了

来个conclusion：So, my cousins house is a place where I love to visit but cant live there.

  • 回答的时候 已经有了提示 你写下几个你记住了的提高分数的相关词汇 然后用他们去回答；过渡连接的词语很重要 比如上面那些：Well, However, Similarly, and so on, Despite, Firstly, Secondly, The most important is, Apart from this, So等等 其实这个有个list的

    你只要记住几个用得最顺手的，说话就有条理了，跟你写作文的时候差不多。
    我知道写作文的时候大多是议论文，议论文的思路其实就是外国人思考的方式了。他们很喜欢先说铺垫一下，然后说一个重点Main Ideas（上面就是my aunt‘s house了。）接着分点去描述这个房子。

    • 无论是什么问题，回答的时候先说Hook一下，Main ideas > Supporting Details > Conclusion
      事实上Speaking，答案写在纸上也是一大篇文章。
      你写作文的时候就是分论点概述，每个自然段通常第一句or最后一句有自己的各自的重点。
      后面都是支持这个ideas的详述，最后来个conclusion 说话跟写文并没有什么差别。
      请灵活运用议论文模板，你要制造一个你自己的说话用的模版 。那回答问题的时候就像填空一样。反正有规律的，你会想到属于你自己的节奏的。我当初就是这么训练自己的。

Pt.3

  1. Do Chinese People like to visit other's homes?

    Yes, people in my country love to invite others as guests. Usually, people like to invite each other for dinners during the holidays. I believe that Chinese culture gives a lot more value to relationships than the western world, which is why people here love to develop strong bonds with their relatives and close friends.

  2. What do Chinese people do when they visit others?

    Basically, we also will make calls to notify the party host before we visit. Have some time to prepare our comings.
    In fact, the most frequent times of our visitation to others happens in the Chinese New Year period which is the Lunar New Year. We will bring gifts for the host, such as Fruit Basket, Snacks, custom special gifts for the new year visit, etc.

    Between our visit, we will have fun together, we like to sit together for dinner, each of us chatting about the daily life about interesting happen, work, news, either happy or unhappy things together.

    After dinner, we like to sit on the sofa watching TV together, have some dessert or tea, kids can play themselves, adults having some more social communications.

  3. What kind of place do people in your country like to live in?

    Well, it depends on whether a person is living in the city or the countryside.
    In small cities, most of the people live in the medium-rise apartment buildings. In the metropolitan cities in China people also live in high-rise aptment buildings; some rich people choose to live in a house in the suburb of the city.
    In the rural areas, people live in houses built out of mud or brick depending upon their financial status.

  4. What's the difference between homes in cities and those in the contryside?

There are many differences between the houses located in posh areas of the city and those located in the outskirts.
The primary difference is the price, the houses in city centres are generally expensive, whereas those in the suburbs are generally more affordable.
Another reason why people move to suburban areas is that because they can build large houses there and these areas are less crowded as compared to city centres.
Naturally, there is ample parking space and less traffic congestion in the suburbs.

在用录音机记录下你的答案的时候，你就可以根据他们的要求寻找你答案上的weakness了，每种都占了25%of the points：

检查&提分

  • Fluency

    1. 答案的长度 越长越好 2.回答的时候有没有忽然停顿了很久（想不到下面说什么的时候）

  • Vocabulary

    展示你用词的范围，越丰富越好。同一个词的意思用不同的词汇说出来。他们在各自句子中的时态有没有错误。

  • Grammar

    • 时态，在同一个句子中结合使用。比如说：What kinda weather do you like.一个普通现在时的问题。 I just love hot weather, last week it was perfect. 使用了一般现在时和一般过去时回答。
    • 句子的结构 你用了多复杂的结构
    • 语法错误 以上都要求你的语法正确才能得分

  • Pronunciation

​ 正确的词汇发音；词汇结尾的轻音；句子有没有没有重音，重音的音调会让你的句子更丰富，突出重点。
